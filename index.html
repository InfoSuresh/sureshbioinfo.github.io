About Me

Background

Pursuing PhD (Bioinformatics) at the National Institute of Immunology, New Delhi, under Dr. Debasisa Mohanty, Director (NII). Expertise in High-throughput analysis, Meta-analysis, Database development, Machine-Learning and various Next Generation Sequencing analysis. The current Doctoral research is on single-cell immune repertoire data analysis. My primary interest is developing pipelines for big data analysis and databases in biological sciences.

With a strong foundation in both bioinformatics and computer science, I develop scalable bioinformatics pipelines and deep learning models that turn large biological datasets into actionable insights for healthcare
